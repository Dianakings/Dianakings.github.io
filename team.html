Meet the Team

Mina Vaughn

Mina Vaughn is an American travel blogger. She is the recipient of several accolades, including an Travellers' Award, and nominations for two Primetime Emmy Awards and a Tony Award. The daughter of Kenyan businessman,Steven Vaughn,was born in Miami, where her father was working, and was raised in Kenya from the age of five.

Sammy Taraji

Sammy Taraji was an American actor. During his two-decade career, Sammy received two Screen Actors Guild Awards, a Golden Globe Award, a Critics' Choice Movie Award, and a Primetime Emmy Award as well as a Travellors' award which resulted to his first meetup with Mina.
